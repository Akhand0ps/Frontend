Through valleys low and mountains high,
Love's beacon guides, never saying goodbye.
In laughter shared and tears unshed,
Love's tender touch forever led.

In every sunrise, in each twilight's hue,
Love's essence glows, forever true.
In simple moments, grand and small,
Love's presence reigns, binding all.

So let us cherish love's gentle sway,
In every moment, come what may.
For in love's embrace, we find our light,
Guiding us through both day and night.
